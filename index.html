About DebateCraft

Our Story

DebateCraft is a nonprofit initiative designed to empower middle and high school students. Our focus is on equipping them with essential skills in argumentation, advocacy, and public speaking. We aim to provide high-quality debate education to under-resourced and underrepresented students, empowering them to excel academically and in life.

Mission Statement

Empowering students

Our mission centers on empowering students by providing them with the tools of debate. Through our programs, we nurture confidence, self-expression, and critical thinking, allowing students to articulate their thoughts and ideas effectively in academic settings and beyond.

Increasing Accessibility

We strive to make debate education accessible to all, especially for students from underrepresented backgrounds. By breaking down barriers, we create opportunities for these students to develop skills that enhance their public speaking and argumentation abilities, leading to increased success in their academic and future professional lives.

Beyond debate rounds

Debate skills extend beyond the classroom. By mastering argumentation and public speaking, students enhance their academic performance and become more confident individuals. They learn to engage in constructive discussions, solve problems creatively, and advocate for themselves and their peers, preparing them for success in all areas of life.

Our Courses

Comprehensive debate education for students of all levels

Curriculum Development

Our curriculum has been thoughtfully crafted in collaboration with experienced educators, advised by former HKU debate coach, World University Debate Finalist, and a debate coach of over 20 years teaching at multiple international schools.

Our dedicated coaching staff, composed of competitive debaters from prestigious institutions, brings a wealth of knowledge and firsthand experience to inspire and educate students.

Available Programs

Introduction to Debate

Level 1 8-11 years old

For those new to debate, we will go all the way back to Socrates and learn about logic, argumentation and refutation so that students develop the essential foundation for debate and critical thinking. We will combine this with public speaking drills so that students will leave the course confident in speaking in front of others and able to speak for long durations by themselves.

Key Features:

  • Foundation in logic and critical thinking
  • Public speaking fundamentals
  • Confidence building exercises
  • Introduction to basic debate concepts

British Parliamentary Debate

Level 2 12-14 years old

For those with some prior debate experience, we will introduce the British Parliamentary debate format (the most common debate format globally) and start going into the nuances of argumentation, characterizing key individuals in a debate motion, and doing regular mock debates.

Key Features:

  • British Parliamentary debate format
  • Advanced argumentation techniques
  • Regular mock debates with feedback
  • Exposure to diverse debate topics

Advanced Debate Techniques

Level 3 15+ years old

For those with extensive debate experience, we will be having advanced university level content lectures based on students' interests and high-level debate rounds with comprehensive feedback on both argumentation, refutation, manner, and strategy.

Key Features:

  • University-level debate content
  • Customized curriculum based on interests
  • High-level debate rounds
  • Comprehensive feedback and coaching

Private Claases

Any Level All ages

A unique opportunity for students aged 8 to 18 to explore the world of debate with a dedicated coach. This free online program exposes participants to everything from foundational concepts and skills to advanced debate strategies tailored to the student's goals.

Key Features:

  • Free online program
  • Interactive lessons and activities
  • Tailored debate Experience
  • Accessible to students of all backgrounds
