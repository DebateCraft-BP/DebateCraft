Our Students in Action

Our Mission

Empowering Students

Our mission centers on students empowering students by providing them with the tools of debate. Through our programs, we nurture confidence, self-expression, and critical thinking.

Increasing Accessibility

We strive to make debate education accessible to all, especially for students from underrepresented backgrounds.

Beyond Debate Rounds

Debate skills extend beyond the classroom. By mastering argumentation and public speaking, students enhance their academic performance and become more confident individuals.

Our Offerings

School Partnerships

Aiming to make debate education accessible to all, we are starting to offer in-person events for students in schools where debate otherwise wouldn't be offered.

Holiday Group Classes

Continuing on the success of our Summer Debate Exposure Series and Winter MasterClasses, we will be announcing free online debate group courses for all levels during school breaks. These courses will be using our unique debate curriculum developed in conjunction with internationally recognized debate coaches.

Free Debate Materials

Debate skills shouldn't be locked behind a pay wall, and we are working on creating original debate content to allow students to take the next step in their debate careers.
