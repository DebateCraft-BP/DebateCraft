Partner With Us

Empowering Hong Kong’s youth together through debate and leadership.

We share your mission of empowering youths in Hong Kong with the confidence to grow as a generation of student change-makers and are inspired by the Youth Leadership Conference's platform for students to network and develop innovative service action plans.

Our hope from day one was to reach and educate a wider audience free of charge, reflecting our belief that every student deserves a voice. A partnership could allow us to bring our global network of student debaters and engaging workshops to co-host community sessions or debate workshops, equipping students with the critical thinking skills needed for a successful future.

A partnership could involve our organization offering free debate lessons aimed at equipping students with the skills and confidence to express their ideas, think critically, and engage in meaningful dialogue. This would allow both of our organizations to uplift students and build the foundation for the next generations of advocates and leaders.

Your organization’s mentorship and experience would help us better understand and reach underserved students, giving them the opportunity to try debate.
