Available Programs

Introduction to Debate

WSDC 8-12 years old

For those new to debate, we will go all the way back to Socrates and learn about logic, argumentation, and refutation so that students develop the essential foundation for debate and critical thinking. We will combine this with public speaking drills so that students will leave the course confident in speaking in front of others and able to speak for long durations by themselves.

Key Features:

  • Foundation in logic and critical thinking
  • Public speaking fundamentals
  • Confidence-building exercises
  • Introduction to basic debate concepts

Debate Accelerator Series

WSDC and BP 13-15 years old or > 1 year of debate experience

For those with some prior debate experience, we will either continue WSDC or introduce the British Parliamentary debate format (the most common debate format globally) and start going into the nuances of argumentation, characterizing key individuals in a debate motion, and doing regular mock debates.

Key Features:

  • Most common debate formats globally
  • Advanced argumentation techniques
  • Regular mock debates with feedback
  • Exposure to diverse debate topics

Advanced Debate

WSDC and BP 16+ years old with > 3 years of debate experience

For those with extensive debate experience, we will be having advanced university-level content lectures based on students' interests and high-level debate rounds with comprehensive feedback on both argumentation, refutation, manner, and strategy.

Key Features:

  • University-level debate content
  • Customized curriculum based on interests
  • High-level debate rounds
  • Comprehensive feedback and coaching

PF Casebuilders

Any level 13+ years old

Public Forum is a prepared debate style prevalent in the US and China. With the expertise of our PF coaches, you will learn how to debate in the PF format, how to cut cards, and more. These skills are highly applicable to any research or policy work. We highly encourage everyone to sign up for this course as it gives students a much stronger content base on global issues ranging from economics to international relations and more through application of rebuttal and impacting.

Key Features:

  • A unique exposure to Public Forum from experienced debaters
  • Access to extensive case files
  • High-level debate rounds
  • Comprehensive feedback and coaching

Bioethics Policymakers

Any level 13+ years old

Bioethics Policymakers introduces students to foundational and contemporary issues at the intersection of biology, medicine, and ethics. Through interactive simulations, case studies, and mini policy labs, participants explore topics such as genetic engineering, artificial intelligence in healthcare, and end-of-life decision-making. Students will develop critical thinking and ethical reasoning skills while learning how to craft and advocate for responsible scientific and medical policy proposals.

Key Features:

  • A unique exposure to bioethics
  • Simulations, case studies, and mini policy labs
  • Preparing students for societal dilemmas
  • Taught by both debate and genetics experts

School Coaching

Any level Any Age

School Coaching offers customized debate and public speaking training designed to elevate student performance in classrooms and competitions. Our experienced coaches collaborate closely with schools to deliver structured workshops, tailored curricula, and continuous mentorship that develop analytical thinking, speech organization, and persuasive communication. Each program is adapted to the school’s goals—whether launching a new debate team, strengthening existing programs, or preparing students for interschool tournaments.

Key Features:

  • Access to our 80+ years of coaching experience
  • Online weekly coaching
  • Debate against debaters worldwide
  • Support for competitions
