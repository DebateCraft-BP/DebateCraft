Debate Programs

Comprehensive debate education for students of all levels—crafted by champions, advised by experts, and tailored to help you find your voice.

What is DebateCraft?

For Parents and Students: Debate is more than just arguing—it is the ultimate framework for critical thinking and articulate expression.

Our curriculum has been thoughtfully crafted in collaboration with experienced educators and is advised by a former HKU debate coach and World University Debate Finalist with over 20 years of experience. Whether you are stepping up to the podium for the very first time or preparing for international championships, our top-tier coaching staff will guide you every step of the way.

Why Debate Matters

Unshakable Confidence

Public speaking is humanity's most common fear. We break down those barriers through structured drills, transforming quiet ideas into commanding, persuasive speeches.

Critical Thinking

Students learn to rapidly process information, identify logical fallacies, and construct airtight arguments—skills that translate directly to academic excellence.

Global Awareness

To win a debate, you must understand the world. Students dive deep into international relations, economics, and social policy, building a profound understanding of global issues.

Available Programs

Introduction to Debate

WSDC Ages 8-12

For those new to debate, we go back to Socrates to learn logic, argumentation, and refutation. Combined with public speaking drills, students will leave confident in speaking in front of others and able to hold the floor for extended durations.

  • Foundation in logic and critical thinking
  • Public speaking fundamentals & confidence building

Accelerator Series

WSDC & BP Ages 13-15

For those with some prior experience (> 1 year). We explore British Parliamentary (the most common format globally) and WSDC, focusing on the nuances of argumentation, characterization, and regular mock debates.

  • Advanced argumentation techniques
  • Regular mock debates with direct feedback

Advanced Debate

WSDC & BP Ages 16+

For extensive experience (> 3 years). University-level content lectures customized to students' interests alongside high-level debate rounds with comprehensive strategic feedback.

PF Casebuilders

  • Highly applicable to research & policy work
