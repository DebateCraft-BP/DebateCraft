Partner With Us

Collaborate with DebateCraft to bring accessible, world-class debate and leadership education to your students and community.

Empowering the Next Generation of Leaders

At DebateCraft, we believe every student deserves a voice. We are actively seeking partnerships with forward-thinking schools, educational institutions, and nonprofit organizations who share our commitment to equipping youth with critical thinking, public speaking, and advocacy skills.

By combining your established community reach with our global network of champion debaters and turnkey curricula, we can create scalable, high-impact programs that uplift underserved students and prepare them for future academic and professional success.

Who We Partner With

For School Administrators

Enhance your school's extracurricular offerings without straining your faculty resources. Whether you are looking to launch a brand-new debate club, integrate structured public speaking into your curriculum, or prepare existing teams for interscholastic tournaments, DebateCraft provides the expertise and coaching staff to make it happen.

  • ✓ Turnkey after-school debate programs
  • ✓ Custom curriculum integration
  • ✓ High-level tournament preparation

For Nonprofits & NGOs

Amplify your organization's impact by adding a proven soft-skills development track to your existing youth services. We rely on your mentorship and community ties to reach underserved students, and in return, we provide free, high-quality debate workshops that build confidence, civic engagement, and leadership.

  • ✓ Co-hosted community workshops
  • ✓ Joint leadership conferences
  • ✓ Accessible skills training for underserved youth
