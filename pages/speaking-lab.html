Speaking Lab

Build controlled delivery with practical drills for pace, tone, and emphasis.

Volume

Project enough to command attention without shouting.

Coaching Notes

    Breath Ladder (3 min)

    Inhale 4, hold 2, exhale 6. Repeat while maintaining posture.

    Pace Contrast (5 min)

    Deliver the same paragraph at rushed, moderate, and strategic pace.

    Emphasis Markup (6 min)

    Underline 5 keywords in your speech and stress only those words.

    Clarity Sprint (4 min)

    Read a complex argument slowly, then at round speed while staying clear.

    Practice Paragraph

    This House would introduce universal basic income for all adults. Your task: weigh fiscal cost against dignity, security, and long-term productivity.
